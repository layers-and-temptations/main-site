Our Delectable Creations

Strawberries & Cream

Layers of vanilla cake, fresh strawberries, and homemade whipped cream create a classic and refreshing experience.

Ingredients: Flour, sugar, eggs, strawberries, cream.

Allergens: Gluten, Dairy, Eggs.

M&M Madness

M&M Madness is a rich chocolate cake layered with velvety vanilla cream and crushed M&M’s, topped with whole M&M’s for a colorful, crunchy treat. Perfect for any celebration or indulgent moment!

Ingredients: Flour, sugar, eggs, blueberries, cream cheese.

Allergens: Gluten, Dairy, Eggs.

Kinder Bueno Dream

Decadent chocolate cake, smooth Nutella filling, and a crunchy Kinder Bueno accent for an elevated chocolate experience.

Ingredients: Flour, sugar, cocoa, mint extract, butter.

Allergens: Gluten, Dairy, Eggs.

Ferrero Rocher

Decadent chocolate cake layered with Nutella cream, topped with a signature Ferrero Rocher for the ultimate indulgence.

Ingredients: Flour, sugar, lavender, honey, butter.

Allergens: Gluten, Dairy, Eggs.

Lotus Biscoff

A delightful blend of caramelized Biscoff biscuits, creamy Biscoff spread, and a moist vanilla cake base.

Ingredients: Flour, sugar, peaches, cinnamon, butter.

Allergens: Gluten, Dairy, Eggs.

Mango Mania

The tropical sweetness of ripe mangoes meets the creamy richness of mango purée and fluffy vanilla cake.

Ingredients: Plant-based flour, sugar, vanilla bean, vegan butter.

Allergens: Gluten (check specific flour). Vegan.

Our Story: Baked with Love

From Passion to Perfection

Layers & Temptations started in a small home kitchen, fueled by a lifelong passion for baking and a dream to spread joy through delicious, handcrafted cupcakes. We believe that the best treats are made with love, the finest ingredients, and a sprinkle of creativity.

Our unique selling points? We use locally sourced ingredients whenever possible, offer a wide range of classic and innovative flavors (including vegan and gluten-free options!), and pour our hearts into every single bake. We're not just a bakery; we're a part of your celebrations.
