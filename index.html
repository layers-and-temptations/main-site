Our Delectable Creations

Strawberry Delight Cup

Experience layers of luscious strawberry compote, light vanilla sponge, and velvety cream. A refreshing classic reinvented.

Ingredients: Flour, sugar, eggs, fresh strawberries, vanilla extract, dairy cream.

Allergens: Gluten, Dairy, Eggs.

Lotus Dream Cup

Discover the irresistible crunch of Lotus Biscoff biscuits nestled between layers of creamy cheesecake and Biscoff spread. A true dream.

Ingredients: Lotus Biscoff biscuits, cream cheese, sugar, dairy cream, Lotus Biscoff spread.

Allergens: Gluten, Dairy. May contain traces of nuts.

Ferrero Fantasy Cup

A luxurious dessert cup featuring the iconic Ferrero Rocher chocolate, hazelnut cream, and delicate wafer pieces.

Ingredients: Ferrero Rocher chocolates, hazelnuts, cocoa, sugar, dairy cream, wafer.

Allergens: Gluten, Dairy, Nuts (Hazelnuts), Soy.

Our Story: Baked with Love

Crafting Sweet Memories

Layers & Temptations was born from a love for creating exquisite dessert experiences. We believe in the magic of combining quality ingredients with artistic presentation to bring you moments of pure indulgence. Each dessert cup is a journey of flavors and textures, crafted with passion.

Our specialty lies in our unique dessert cups, perfect for any occasion. We are dedicated to using fresh, high-quality ingredients to ensure every bite is a delight. From vibrant strawberry swirls to rich chocolatey depths, there's a temptation for everyone.
